Windsurfschool information

With two windsurfing centers on the beach, 'surfschool Zandvoort' and 'surfschool Scheveningen', you are not short of choice for your next great windsurfing session. Both centers are equipped with the latest range of windsurfing equipment for beginners and experts. We have the latest and best equipment and we will have you sailing back and forth within 90 minutes! Because we (mostly) teach private lessons, we can adapt the lesson to your needs and level. This allows very fast learning. Minimum age 6 years. You decide which day and the time you want a lesson for beginners, advanced riders or expert riders.

Beginner

As a beginner you will learn within 90 minutes to sail back and forth in your first lesson. After you have done one separate lesson of 90 minutes you can decide to continue with the course.
1 person: € 35.00 including surf gear.

Advanced

Advanced surfers use this lesson to improve their technique in 4 hours using footstraps, harness and learning how to get planing. 1 person: € 125.00 including surf gear

Expert

If you are already a good windsurfer but want to improve on special topics like wave riding, various loops, race techniques or any other moves, we are here to coach you in an efficient and fun way. Together with our coach we determine your needs and wishes before you go sailing together and practice on the water. Price depends on wishes.
