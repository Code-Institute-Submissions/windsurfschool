Zandvoort

Zandvoort on the West Coast is an exposed beach break that has inconsistent surf. Summer in particular tends to be flat. Works best in offshore winds from the east. Most of the surf here comes from groundswells and the best swell direction is from the north northwest. Waves at the beach break both left and right. Good surf at all stages of the tide.

Scheveningen

Most well know spot in Holland, is a stretch of small jettys flanked by a huge harbour wall that gives SW wind protection and a surfing out channel. Often lacks in power and closes out, therefore a lot of windsurfers. Picks up all swell directions and breaks through the tide but best on a NW at high.
